## ARTICLE 6.6<br/>DISCHARGING FIREARMS

```
No person shall fire or discharge any gun, fowling-piece,
or firearm within two hundred feet of any street in the
town of Brookline or on any private grounds, except with
the consent of the owner thereof; provided, however, that
this by-law shall not apply to the use of such weapons at
any military exercise, in law enforcement or in the lawful
defense of the person, family, or property of any citizen.
```
