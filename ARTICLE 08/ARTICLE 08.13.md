## ARTICLE 8.13<br/>INFLAMMABLE MATERIAL

```
The Fire Department, after consultation with or in response
to initiation from the Conservation Commission, shall have
the power, subject to the approval of the Selectmen, to
promulgate rules and regulations governing the issuance and
renewal of any permit or license issued, or the renewal of
any permit or license issued or renewed in accordance with
the laws of the Commonwealth, including the provisions of
Chapter 148 of the General Laws, relative to the storage of
fuel oil, liquid chemicals and/or gasoline.
```
