## ARTICLE 8.1<br/>ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES

```
No person shall have any alcoholic beverage in an open
container or shall drink any alcoholic beverage (as defined
in G.L. c.138, ยง1, as amended) while in or upon any park,
playground or other public place in the town or while in or
upon any private land or place in the town without the
consent of the owner or person having control thereof.  All
alcoholic beverages being used in violation of this section
shall be seized and safely held by the police until final
disposition of the charge against the person or persons
charged with violating this section, at which time they
shall be returned to the person or persons entitled to
possess them.
```
