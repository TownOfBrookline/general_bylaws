## ARTICLE 7.1<br/>ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES

```
No persons shall drink any alcoholic beverage, as defined
in General Laws Chapter 138, ยง1, as amended, while in or
upon any street or sidewalk.  All alcoholic beverages being
used in violation of this Section shall be seized and
safely held by the police until final disposition of the
charge against the person or persons charged with violating
this Section, at which time they shall be returned to the
person or persons entitled to possess them.
```
